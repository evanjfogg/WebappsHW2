Full Stack Application: Ecommerce Website

Evan Fogg
Posted on: April 4, 2020 at 11:50 PM

I plan to create an ecommerce website where customers can sort items by category and purchase them.

Abstract

I am planning to make this a MERN stack application. MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, and NodeJS will be used. The application will allow customers to make purchases and possibly allow specific users to upload new items to be sold. The target audience would be anyone who is interested in having a full stack website to sell from.

Background Information

My goal is to develop a full stack application. My reasoning for choosing an ecommerce website is because it is an application that applies to the real world more than anything. Sellers often need a way to sell their items online, and the demand is only growing for such applications.

Features

Basic features will include an online shopping cart, sorting items by category, being able to pay through the website, and uploading new items as the seller.
